Here's everything you need to make your stay comfortable:

🍳 Kitchen Ready

Our kitchen is fully stocked with all the gadgets. Remember to clean up after use!

Adjust the thermostat in the living room to keep warm. Extra blankets are in the closet for those extra chilly nights.

Wi-Fi is available throughout the cabin - you'll find the password on the fridge. There's also a smart TV for your Netflix binges. Just be sure to log in with your account.

Please keep the cabin and our environment clean by using the trash bins provided.
