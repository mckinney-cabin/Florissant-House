Welcome to Our Family's Special Getaway!

This cabin is filled with memories and equipped with everything we need for a comfortable stay. Here's a quick guide:

The Kitchen

Feel at home in our fully equipped kitchen. You'll find everything from a fridge to a coffee maker. Please clean up after use, so it's nice and tidy for the next family member.

Heat

For those chilly nights, adjust the thermostat in the living room. Extra blankets are in the bedroom – feel free to snuggle up!

Connected and Entertained

Stay connected with Wi-Fi (password on the fridge) and enjoy family movie nights with our smart TV and board games.

Keeping It Clean

Please use the bins for waste and remember, our septic system is a bit old-fashioned – so use toilet paper sparingly.

Fun!!!

Enjoy the patio on the nice days! There is a table and grilling supplies in the shed.

There are board games, cards, and dominos to have a nice family game night!

The yard makes for a great sled hill, just be sure to find a spot without trees.

Don't forget to check out the sleds in the log cabin shed for some snowy adventures!
