Leaving...

As our family time comes to an end, here are some reminders to help us keep the cabin cozy and inviting for our next get-together:

  • The Nest cameras need a charge with the charger in the end table next to the couch. When returning to dock, check with Lisa they are positioned correctly.
  • Please take a moment to clean and put away the dishes.
  • Wash used linens, there is a laundromat in Woodland Park.
  • A sweep with the vacuum keeps our floors looking nice.
  • Check the fridge for perishables and take out the trash.
  • Don't forget to leave a note or a fun drawing in our family journal on the entertainment center. It's always a joy to read your thoughts and stories!
  • Turn off the lights.
  • A quick path clearing of snow makes it safer for the next family members arriving.
  • Check the windows and doors, ensuring they're all shut and locked.
  • Lock up the storage shed.

Seasonal Reminders

  • In spring, summer, and early fall, please store trash in the storage shed.
  • If you're the last one out during winter or as winter approaches, discuss winterizing the cabin with Jimmy.
  • Pour RV antifreeze in all of the drains and toilet if the heat is to be shut down in the cabin.
  • Discuss the furnace settings with Jimmy before you leave.
  • Discuss the propane with Jimmy before you leave.

Consider replinishing if low.

  • Toilet paper.
  • Paper towels.
  • RV Antifreeze.
  • Laundry pods and Dryer sheets.
  • Coffee and filters.
  • Hot chocolate.
  • Peppermint Schnapps.
