As We Say Goodbye...

As our family time comes to an end, here are some reminders to help us keep the cabin cozy and inviting for our next get-together:

  • Please take a moment to clean and put away the dishes. It's like leaving a little love for the next family visit!
  • Wash used linens, there is a laundromat in Woodland Park.
  • Double-check the windows and doors, ensuring they're all shut and locked.
  • Remember to lock up the storage shed.
  • A quick path clearing of snow makes it safer for the next family members arriving.
  • A sweep with the vacuum keeps our floors looking nice.
  • Before you leave, check the fridge for perishables and take out the trash.
  • The Nest cameras just need a quick charge with the charger in the end table next to the couch. When returning to dock, check with Lisa they are positioned correctly.
  • Turn off the lights.
  • Don't forget to leave a note or a fun drawing in our family journal on the entertainment center. It's always a joy to read your thoughts and stories!

Seasonal Reminders

  • In spring, summer, and early fall, please store trash in the storage shed. It's our little contribution to keeping our natural surroundings pristine.
  • If you're the last one out as winter approaches or during winter, discuss with Jimmy winterizing the cabin.
