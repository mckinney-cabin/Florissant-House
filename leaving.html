Preparing for Leaving the Cabin

  • Please wash, dry, and put away all dishes used during your stay. This helps maintain cleanliness and ensures everything is ready for the next guests.
  • Please wash linens prior to departure. While we do not have laundry facilities on-site, there is a local laundromat nearby for your convenience.
  • Lower the thermostat: Set the heating to a low but safe level to prevent pipes from freezing.
  • Secure all windows and doors: Ensure they're locked and sealed to prevent drafts and protect against the elements.
  • Turn off water supply: To avoid freezing, turn off the main water supply and drain the system (See winterization below).
  • Unplug appliances: Disconnect all non-essential electrical devices to prevent hazards and save energy.
  • Clear snow from exits: Make sure paths and exits are free of snow for safety.
  • Store food and dispose of trash: Remove perishables and take out the garbage to avoid attracting wildlife.
