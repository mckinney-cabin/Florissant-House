Here are some of our local favorites!

Destinations

    Eleven Mile State Canyon: (10 min) Fishing, hiking, and picnicing.

    Mueller State Park: (10 min) Trails, sledding, and wildlife.

    Breckenridge Ski area: (1.5-2 hr) Ski resort $$$, endless of terrain.

    Keystone Ski area: (2-2.5 hr) Ski resort $$$, endless terrain.

    Ski Cooper Ski area: (2-2.5 hr) Ski resort $, affordable and no crowds.

    Florissant Fossil Beds National Monument: (5 min) Nice hikes.

    Garden of the Gods: (1 hr) Hikes around rock monuments.

Restaurants

    Judges Char-grill - Woodland Park: (15 min) American and Mexican dishes, family meals or watching a game.

    Thunderbird Inn - Florissant: (5 min) Burgers, live music, and drinks.

    Java Haus - Woodland Park: (15 min) Coffee, pastries, and welcoming environment, ideal for a quick coffee break.

    McGinty's - Divide: (10 min) A unique pub with wood-fired pizzas, Irish specialties, and a variety of beers and spirits.

    Bierwerks Brewery - Woodland Park: (15 min) A local favorite brewery.
