Local Favorites

Destinations

  • Eleven Mile State Canyon: (10 min) Enjoy fishing, hiking, and picnicing in a scenic environment.
  • Mueller State Park: (10 min) Offers beautiful trails, sledding, and wildlife watching opportunities.
  • Breckenridge Ski area: (1.5-2 hr) A popular ski resort known for its vast skiable terrain and vibrant town.
  • Keystone Ski area: (2-2.5 hr) Renowned for its family-friendly slopes and night skiing.
  • Ski Cooper Ski area: (2-2.5 hr) Offers a unique and affordable skiing experience, great for families and those seeking less crowded slopes.
  • Florissant Fossil Beds National Monument: (5 min) Explore the rich ancient history and preserved fossils of Colorado.
  • Garden of the Gods: (1 hr) A natural landmark featuring stunning sandstone rock formations, offering hiking trails, rock climbing, and breathtaking views for a memorable outdoor experience.
